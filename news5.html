Acts of Magis: Ateneans in the Service of Society featuring “Access to Justice under COVID-19”

2020-06-30

The Ateneo De Manila University Research Council (URC) together with the Ateneo Research Institute of Science and Engineering (ARISE) invite you to the Webinar Acts of Magis: Ateneans in the Service of Society

featuring:

"Access to Justice under COVID-19"

on 3 July 2020 at 4:00 pm

Webinar Link

facebook.com/ateneodemanila

Speaker: Atty. Ray Paolo J. Santiago

Abstract:

Atty. Santiago will discuss the various issues related to access to legal information and possible remedies as they apply to the vulnerable and marginalized sectors of society especially at this time of the pandemic.

--

Atty. Santiago is the Executive Director of the Ateneo Human Rights Center and faculty member of the Ateneo Law School. He is the Secretary-General of the Working Group for an ASEAN Human Rights Mechanism, a recognized entity in the ASEAN Charter.

Atty Santiago was a recipient of the Freedom Flame Award of the Friedrich Naumann Foundation in 2014 and the inaugural ASEAN Peoples Award in 2015.

We are looking forward to your participation in the webinar.

For any questions, please email urc@ateneo.edu.
